About this map

What you're looking at: conflict pressure on school-age children (5-14) across Burkina Faso's 17 regions.

  • Choropleth (yellow → red): events per 100,000 children — a population-normalised intensity that compares regions of different sizes fairly.
  • Black triangles: recent displacement events (3 Nov 2025 → 15 Mar 2026), sized by people displaced.
  • Dark blue dots: schools mapped in OpenStreetMap (partial coverage).
  • Permanent labels: the 17 regions of Burkina Faso (post-2025 reorganisation).

Period: 1 April 2025 → 30 April 2026
Sources: ACLED · IDMC · UNFPA 2023 · OpenStreetMap · OCHA / IGB

Tip: click any region to zoom in. Use the ↺ button (top-left) to return to the country view.
